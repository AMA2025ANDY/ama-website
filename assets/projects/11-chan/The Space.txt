The interior is conceived as a dynamic, masculine space, its furniture tactile and feminine. The seating is arranged in a central sculptural element that floats within the charcoal anodized box. Intricately crafted, luxurious semi-private booths — for two or for larger groups — make Chan cosy and intimate. A dramatic sense of layering runs through the space: graphically lit wall panels, open grid ceilings, engraved detailing on the tables, tattooed graphics on the upholstery, and 21st-century "manga" interpretations in the cocktail lounge — an experience of Asian street culture and cosmopolitan sophistication.
