A ‘pan-Asian’ restaurant and bar at ‘The Met’ hotel Thessaloniki, Greece, is a lively fusion of modern aesthetic and traditional Asian motifs. The internal dining space is 230sqm and 150sqm for external dining in the hotels courtyard. Access to the restaurant via discreet entrances from the hotel and the street leads into the impressive and softly lit venue. ama extensively researched their brief from ‘The Met’ with trips to Hong Kong, Thailand and Indonesia, trying to seek out the essence of distinctly modern Asian cuisine.  The aim was to create a truly sumptuous atmosphere without the stereotypical visual references. The contrast between traditional and contemporary Asian culture and the duality that exists between the two were integrated into the design. The restaurant’s interior is conceived as a dynamic masculine space, with its furniture tactile and feminine.
