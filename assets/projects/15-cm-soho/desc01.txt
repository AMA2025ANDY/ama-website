Chotto Matte Soho brings the essence of modern Tokyo to London’s vibrant Frith Street. Housed within an imposing 1960s building, the 450-square-meter restaurant and bar serves as the flagship location for the brand's signature Japanese-Peruvian Nikkei cuisine. Designed by London-based Andy Martin Architects (ama), the space is conceived as a hybrid of high-end luxury and raw underground energy, offering a sumptuous dining experience deliberately devoid of stereotypical traditional visual references.
