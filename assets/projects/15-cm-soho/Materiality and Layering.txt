The architectural philosophy heavily relies on the concept of "layering"—a nod to the complex visual and cultural fabric of Tokyo. Rather than overt traditional motifs, the design creates drama through contrasting textures and commissioned collaborations. Vibrant street art is protected behind bespoke cast-glass wall panels, while natural Lavastone elements are illuminated to glow with vivid color, bridging the gap between raw urban grit and polished architectural execution.
