Barrafina is an homage to the small, familiar, and vibrant traditional tapas bars of Spain, meticulously translated for the London dining scene. Across its locations, including its expansions into Borough Yards and beyond, the design philosophy remains fiercely consistent: celebrating the kinetic energy of a bustling service. The inherently compact nature of the restaurant space is not a limitation but a deliberate design choice, generating an immediate, infectious sense of activity the moment guests step through the doors.
