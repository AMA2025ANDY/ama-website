The vocabulary is soft and rounded throughout: walls that curve out to meet the floor, lozenge-shaped ceiling lights in the ground-floor bar, and a pebble-dash wall finish in the staircase area — all contributing to the cartoon-like feel. Custom furniture runs to chrome tulip-base tables, timber-and-"integral-skin"-polyurethane dining chairs, and S-shaped upholstered banquettes. A back-lit photo-mural and the orange "blob" signage and apertures carry the colour.
