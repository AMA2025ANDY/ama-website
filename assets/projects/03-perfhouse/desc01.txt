Perf House began with a question of light. The clients, from Sardinia, wanted to bring something of that island's openness and brightness into the heart of London. Working with Andy Martin Architecture, the project stripped a petit five-storey Georgian terrace back to nothing, removing every internal element to leave a single six-sided volume. Into that emptied shell ama re-imagined a new home built on transparency and connection, materiality and texture, light and shadow, excitement and tranquility. The name comes from the perforated steel spine that rises through the centre of the house, the move that lets light fall the full height of the plan.
