The overarching material palette relies on a muted, seamless restraint, which serves to amplify moments of extreme tectonic density. In the washroom, the vanity is executed as a brutalist, monolithic block of heavily figured, dark green stone. This heavy geological insertion grounds the ethereal, softly lit space. Conversely, in the adjacent study area, the architectural intervention shifts to strict, linear precision. A bespoke, wall-to-wall shelving system is constructed from a rigorous grid of slender raw steel plates intersecting with warm, richly grained timber cabinetry, imposing a sudden geometric order against the organic fluidity of the neighboring stairwell.
