Vertical circulation is resolved through a sweeping, sculptural staircase that functions as the tectonic core of the residence. The solid, monolithic balustrade winds around a central structural volume clad entirely in narrow, vertically stacked glazed ceramic tiles. The high-gloss, subtly undulating surface of these tiles reacts dynamically to the natural light spilling from the overhead skylight, establishing a tactile, luminous counterpoint to the matte, seamless micro-cement finishes applied to the surrounding floors and walls.
