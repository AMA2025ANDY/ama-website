A central entry axis divides a garage and facilities block to the north from the main home to the south, which opens into a double-height living, kitchen and dining area. A large gable-end window in the living space overlooks a newly created 400-square-metre pond, introduced to attract local wildlife and birds. Folding doors open the dining area onto a south terrace, and a run of timber decking extends out over the pond from the garden — for cold-water swimming or simply being in nature.
