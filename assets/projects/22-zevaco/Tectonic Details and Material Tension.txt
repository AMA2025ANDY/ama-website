The interior fit-out is defined by a rigorous material palette that balances mid-century nostalgia with precise, contemporary detailing. A defining feature is the circulation core, where a staircase is detailed with heavy blue-stone treads, an aggressive zigzagging brass stringer, and a structural glass balustrade anchored by a sculptural metallic newel post. This high-contrast materiality continues in the transitional spaces, where backlit, textured glass-block walls are juxtaposed against dark, monolithic flooring and intricate, chaotic geometric screens that echo Zevaco’s original 1950s aesthetic.
