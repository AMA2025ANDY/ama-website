Located at the prestigious 161 Bay Street, Chotto Matte Toronto marks the brand's third global outpost and its highly anticipated debut in Canada. Spanning a massive 10,000 square feet, the restaurant brings the vibrant, avant-garde energy of modern Tokyo to the heart of Toronto’s Financial District. Designed by long-time brand collaborator Andy Martin Architecture (AMA), the space is a striking architectural achievement that marries Chotto Matte’s signature Japanese-Peruvian Nikkei dining experience with sweeping, sculptural design.
