To bring the brand's signature urban grit to the sculptural elements, the design team collaborated with internationally renowned street artist Lefty Out There (under the art direction of Marcel Katz). Transforming the architectural ribbons into a massive 3D canvas, the artist hand-painted vibrant, graffiti-like murals directly onto the fabric and the surrounding walls. This explosive artwork is beautifully contrasted by a warm, slatted wood ceiling above the bar, which was meticulously scribed to seamlessly meet the back of the ribbons.
