While other locations rely on stone or glass to anchor the room, the Toronto location is defined by its ceiling landscape. The interior is dominated by a series of monumental, intertwining architectural "ribbons." Engineered and fabricated by Eventscape, three independent, 154-foot-long aluminium tubular frames clad in pillow-cased fabric playfully crisscross the narrow space. These sweeping structures wrap around building columns, navigate HVAC equipment, and interact directly with the architecture—starting and stopping at the central steel staircase, with one ribbon dramatically dipping down to meet the floor in a stainless steel shoe.
