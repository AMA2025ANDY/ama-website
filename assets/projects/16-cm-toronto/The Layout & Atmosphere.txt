Designed to accommodate up to 425 guests in total, the expansive layout ensures a highly kinetic dining experience. The heart of the room features an open kitchen with a dynamic robata grill counter and sushi bar, allowing guests to witness the culinary theatrics firsthand. The venue also introduced Chotto Matte’s first-ever dedicated cocktail lounge, AkACHAN (translating to "baby" in Japanese), which features nightly DJs, and is complemented by a lush, 150-seat heated outdoor terrace for year-round dining.
