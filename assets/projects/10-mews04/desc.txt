Mews 4 is a refurbishment to a 5-bedroom house in London. The initial brief was to redesign the timber-framed conservatory designed in the 80's in the Victorian style. The solution was to replace it with a similar form so as to not have to submit a potentially laborious planning application. The original design of the 3-barrel vaults was transformed into a contemporary sine curve form, with 3 large curved double glazed units, the largest single horizontal units in the UK.
