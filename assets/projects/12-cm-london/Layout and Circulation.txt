A formal entrance off Frith Street opens into a reception lobby, from which guests can enter the bar and sushi bar or take a spiral stair up to the first-floor restaurant. The ground floor holds a cocktail lounge with retractable glass walls that open the room to the street, alongside a raised dining area and a fixed-price raw bar; a spiral burnt-timber staircase leads up to an open Robata grill, sushi bar and a larger dining room above.
